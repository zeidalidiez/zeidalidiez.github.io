About Me

With prior experience in tech project management as well as IT, I specialize in the following:

  • Bridging the communication gap between experts and end-users.
  • Taking charge of seeing all aspects of a project come to completion
  • Engaging in thorough research and logic checks
  • Maintaining an open line of communication with all moving parts of a task

Get in touch with me about how we can work together to build something exciting!

Located in Auburn, WA. I am close to Seattle and Tacoma.

I love learning and seeking new experiences.

I have spent all of my life fiddling around with scripting as a hobby for various videogames and modifications of existing games. I love to 'take things apart' and figure out how they work - they're all fun logic puzzles.
